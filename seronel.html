Formulation Structure

Built around barrier lipids, hydration-binding agents, and calming botanical support — optimized for comfort and daily stability.

  • Ceramide NP: Supports the skin’s lipid barrier structure to help reduce dryness-related discomfort.
  • Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5): Helps soothe and condition the skin while supporting moisture retention.
  • Betaine: A gentle osmolyte that helps balance hydration and reduce tight, stressed feel.
  • Centella Components: Calming botanical support commonly used to reduce visible irritation and support comfort.
  • Hydration Retention System: A moisture-layering approach designed to hold water and keep finish non-drying over time.
