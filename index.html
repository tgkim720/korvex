The KORVEX Platform

KORVEX is a specialized distribution infrastructure designed to bridge the gap between premium Korean dermocosmetics and global professional markets.

Distribution Infrastructure
We build the legal and logistical framework for seamless export, prioritizing compliance and stable supply chains over simple product listing.
Compliance Management
From MSDS to NPRA notifications, we manage all necessary documentation to reduce regulatory friction and protect our partners' import rights.
Curated Portfolio
We exclusively manage a high-performance portfolio of Korean clinic-grade brands, ensuring each product meets professional efficacy standards.

Strategic Partnership

We go beyond supply. We provide the tools, flexibility, and expertise required for clinics and distributors to scale successfully.

🚀

Starter-Friendly Tiers

Lowering the barrier for market entry. We offer Starter Pack options and low MOQ tiers, allowing clinics to test market response without heavy inventory commitment.

Clinical Protocols

Operational Agility
