FAQ
What ordering options are available?

Three options depending on your setup. FOB Korea for distributors managing their own freight — maximize unit economics, arrange your own logistics. Door delivery fulfillment for clinics who want stock shipped direct to their address without handling freight. Evaluation orders for first-time partners who want to validate the product before committing to volume. Pricing for each is provided after your first message.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No fixed MOQ barrier. Evaluation orders are available from the first order. Volume tiers apply as quantities scale — larger orders access better unit pricing. Mix SKUs to reach a tier.

What documentation is included?

Full export doc pack provided: INCI list, Certificate of Analysis (COA), MSDS, product spec sheets, and clinical data. Regulatory documentation for US FDA MoCRA and EU CPNP registration also available on request.

What certifications does the product line carry?

Mostly US FDA MoCRA registered · EU CPNP registered · ISO 22716 GMP certified manufacturing. Clinical trial data available — documented improvements in skin texture and barrier recovery.

What are the shipping terms?

Standard terms are FOB Korea — we handle export dispatch and documentation from Korea, freight to your destination is arranged by the buyer. For partners who prefer direct delivery, our fulfillment service ships to your clinic or warehouse address. Reach out with your destination for a quote.

How many SKUs are available?

20+ SKUs across recovery, barrier restoration, hydration, and brightening. We recommend starting with 2–4 core SKUs to test market response before broadening the range.

Which markets are you currently active in?

Active in over 13 export markets across Asia, Europe, and North America. Reach out with your target market — we'll confirm availability and any existing channel conflicts before moving forward.

Can I get market exclusivity?

Available for discussion depending on volume commitment and market size. Reach out to talk terms.

What is the typical lead time?

Most SKUs are available in ready stock. For large-volume orders, lead time varies by scale — contact us first and we'll confirm timeline before you commit.

Is white-label or OEM available?

Yes. Beyond our existing clinic-grade lines, we can connect you with suitable Korean manufacturers for ODM and OEM production. Reach out with your brief and we'll take it from there.
