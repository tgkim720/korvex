Active Systems in This Line

Selected Active Systems

Each formula is built around a focused active system selected for post-procedure use, barrier support, and clinic homecare compatibility.

Exosome (Cica)
Cica-derived exosome at 10,000ppm — nano-sized vesicles for deep barrier penetration and regeneration. Core active in Exo-Cica Recovery. Supports texture refinement and post-procedure recovery.
ALL EGF+
Full growth factor complex (EGF, IGF, FGF) — stimulates cellular renewal and tissue repair. Anti-wrinkle clinical trials completed at K-Skin Clinical Center. Present in Exo-Cica Recovery and Azulene Repair Cream.
PDRN
Polynucleotide complex at 5,000ppm — supports barrier density restoration and tissue revitalization. Present in PDRN Luminous Cream, PDRN Sun Defense, and PDRN Elasticity Restore Booster.
CICA-7 Complex
3-stage centella asiatica in Blemish Recovery Balm. Tri-phase delivery calms post-procedure inflammation at different skin depths while providing hypoallergenic coverage.
ABP-15%
AHA + BHA + PHA triple acid at 15% in Phyto-Glow Peel. Professional exfoliation concentration adapted for daily home use with Botanical 8-System irritation buffering.
Spicules 99%
99% high-purity sea sponge microcrystal needles in Active Synergy Booster. Physical micro-stimulation mechanism that replicates in-clinic MTS topically — less irritation than needle-based treatments.
Tranexamic Acid Alpha 5™
Proprietary upgraded tranexamic acid complex in Melanin-Block Tone Cream. Targets all 3 stages of melanin production simultaneously — more comprehensive than standard tranexamic acid formulations.
Vit-Tree / Sea Buckthorn
Sea buckthorn (vitamin tree) fruit water at 780,000ppm as the base in Vit-Tree Radiance Booster — naturally concentrated antioxidant and vitamin source targeting hyperpigmentation at the deeper skin layer.
Propolis Complex
Honey-derived propolis at 580,000ppm in Propolis-Bond Barrier Cream and coating-film mechanism in Propolis Seal Ampoule. Seals barrier moisture while delivering anti-inflammatory bioactives.
Aquaporin System (Aqua GG)
Patented barrier-strengthening complex in Deep Channel Barrier Toner. Activates aquaporin water channels for deeper moisture transport than standard hyaluronic acid alone — 97% specialist satisfaction rate.
Lactic Acid Fermentation
Live fermentation-derived lactic acid in Lacto-Mild Cleansing Gel. Cleanses at physiological pH while supplementing moisture — maintains the skin's acid mantle rather than disrupting it like alkaline foaming cleansers.
WTS-10000™
Proprietary penetration-enhancing delivery base in Active Synergy Booster. Amplifies absorption of all active products applied after treatment — designed for home-use protocol sequencing.
