Post-Procedure Recovery

Laser · RF · IPL

After laser, RF, or energy-based procedures, the skin barrier is compromised and reactive. This protocol uses Vitamin K synergy combined with PDRN-based regeneration to accelerate barrier restoration, suppress redness, and extend treatment results at home.

Timing Product Instructions
Day 0–3 Vita-K Repair Mask Apply immediately post-procedure. Vitamin K complex for instant soothing; ceramide seals compromised barrier. 15–20 min daily for first 3 days.
Day 1–14 Vita-K Repair Cream AM/PM application. Reduces residual redness, accelerates recovery. Suitable for sensitive and reactive skin post-procedure.
Day 7+ PDRN Regenerative Serum Introduce once acute phase subsides. High-purity PDRN + NMN for deeper regeneration and elasticity recovery. AM and PM.
Daily (AM) Active Protection Sun Serum SPF50+ Non-negotiable for post-laser skin. Non-sticky, non-white cast. Triple function: UV protection + brightening + wrinkle improvement.
